Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Rocket Floor Pros ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information obtained from visitors to this website (rocketfloorpros.com).

Important Notice: This website is operated by an independent lead generation service. When you submit a form on this site, your information may be shared with licensed epoxy floor coating contractors in your area who may contact you regarding your estimate request.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Property address
  • Project details (type of project, timeline, property ownership status)
  • Any additional notes you include in your message

We also collect standard website analytics data through tools like Google Analytics, including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and general geographic location (city/region level).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Connect you with licensed floor coating contractors in your service area
  • Follow up on your estimate request
  • Improve our website and services
  • Send you relevant information about epoxy floor coating services (if you have opted in)

Sharing Your Information

When you submit a form requesting an estimate, your contact information and project details may be shared with one or more licensed epoxy floor coating contractors in your area who will contact you to provide a quote. By submitting the form, you consent to being contacted by these contractors.

We do not sell your personal information to third-party marketers. We do not share your information with companies unrelated to your estimate request.

Phone and Text Communication

By providing your phone number and submitting a form, you consent to receive phone calls and/or text messages from our network of contractors regarding your estimate request. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to analyze website traffic and improve your experience. We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can opt out of Google Analytics by visiting Google's opt-out page.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at the phone number or address provided on our contact page.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
