About Rocket Floor Pros

North Alabama's trusted garage floor coating specialists since 2019.

OUR STORY

Born and Built in North Alabama

Rocket Floor Pros was founded in 2019 with one goal: to bring professional-grade epoxy and polyaspartic floor coatings to North Alabama homeowners at honest prices. What started as a small operation out of Huntsville has grown into one of the region's most trusted floor coating services, with over 500 completed projects across Huntsville, Madison, Decatur, Athens, and surrounding communities.

We're not a national franchise. We're locals who live and work in this community. Every project we take on, we treat the way we'd treat our own home. That means showing up on time, communicating clearly, doing thorough concrete prep, and leaving your garage spotless when we're done.

Our name is a nod to the Rocket City — Huntsville, Alabama. Just like the engineers at NASA's Marshall Space Flight Center who built on precision and attention to detail, we build floors that perform under pressure and look great for decades.
